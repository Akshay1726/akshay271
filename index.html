Vignan UEAC

by Akshay on August 15, 2023

The UEAC Unit of VFSTR was established in the year 2009 to motivate students towards community-oriented service. These activities are aimed at instilling social consciousness and responsibility among budding engineers. They also enable VFSTR to forge strong links with the community and help in its development through various extension activities. The unit also encourages community involvement in the college's growth and development.
